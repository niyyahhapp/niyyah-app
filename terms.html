About NIYYAH

NIYYAH is a Qur'an understanding and reflection app.

Educational Purpose

NIYYAH is an educational and reflection tool. It is not a scholar, mufti, fatwa service, religious authority, therapist, medical provider, legal advisor, or emergency service.

Qur'an Text and Tafsir

NIYYAH must not alter Arabic Qur'an text. Translations, tafsir summaries, and learning notes are provided for understanding and reflection.

User Reflections

Users may write private reflections, actions, and duas in the app. In the first version, these entries are stored locally on the user's device.

No Guarantee

NIYYAH is provided as-is. We aim to build a thoughtful, accurate, and safe product, but we cannot promise the app will always be error-free or available.
