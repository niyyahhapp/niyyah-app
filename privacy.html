Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026. This is a founder draft and should be reviewed before App Store launch.

Overview

NIYYAH is a Qur'an understanding and reflection app designed to help users read, understand, reflect, and journal one ayah at a time.

Information We Collect

In the first app version, NIYYAH is designed to work without login and without a backend. The app may store reflections, duas, favorite ayahs, vocabulary words, memorization progress, completed ayahs, and streaks locally on the user's device.

Waitlist Information

If a user joins the NIYYAH waitlist, we collect their email address through our email/waitlist provider for launch updates, early access invitations, and product progress updates.

Religious Content

NIYYAH provides Qur'an text, translation, source tafsir, and beginner-friendly learning notes. NIYYAH does not replace qualified scholars, teachers, or personal religious guidance.

Data Sharing

NIYYAH does not sell personal data. Waitlist emails may be processed by our email/waitlist provider.
