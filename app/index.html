Surah Al-Ikhlas 112:1

قُلْ هُوَ ٱللَّهُ أَحَدٌ

Say, He is Allah, One.

Simple Meaning

Allah teaches the Prophet ﷺ to declare that Allah is One, unique, and unlike anything in creation.

What This Teaches Us

Faith begins with knowing who Allah is and keeping worship sincerely for Him alone.

Source summary: Tafsir Ibn Kathir, beginner-friendly wording.
